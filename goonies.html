About The Goonies

The Goonies is a 1980's movie. It is a movie everyone can watch and enjoy. The movie is the story of two brothers who are going to lose their house, but the youngest brother want to save it. The youngest brother, Mikey, finds a treasure map that he believed would solve the problem with his house. He brings some friends along and they all begin an adventure, which also lead into danger. But Goonies never give up.

CHARACTERS

In the movie there are seven Kids who are running away from bad guys. The kid's names are Mikey, Stef, Mouth, Andy, Brand, Chunk, and Data. The bad guys are bothers and their names are Francis and Jake. The bad guy's leader is their mom, Mama Fratelli.
